About

Chasing Light Across
the Hawaiian Islands

Pixel Pacific Media is a Hawaii-based fine art photography studio specializing in astrophotography, volcanic landscapes, and coastal imagery across the Hawaiian island chain.

Every image begins with a journey — hiking into darkness before dawn, waiting hours for the Milky Way core to rise, or standing at the edge of an active lava field at midnight. The result is photography that captures Hawaii's raw, otherworldly beauty.

All prints are produced on museum-quality media with archival inks, available in multiple substrates including Lustre, Metallic, Fine Art Matte, and Acrylic Face Mount.
