Loop Phone Booths

Acoustic privacy pods that transform any open office into a focused, productive workspace.

← All Manufacturers

Privacy pods built for the way people actually work.

Loop Phone Booths designs and manufactures acoustic office privacy pods — from single-person phone booths to four-person meeting pods — using composite materials optimized for superior acoustic performance. Each booth ships 100% pre-assembled and is UL 962 Listed, with hundreds of color and finish combinations available to match any interior. Designed in Edmonton, Canada, and trusted by Google, Disney, Shopify, Pokémon, and companies across Northern California.

Beyond pods, Loop's furniture collection extends the brand's refined, customizable aesthetic to lounge seating, chairs, and planters — creating a cohesive, productive environment from floor to ceiling.

Product Line

Loop Solo

The one-person privacy pod that makes a statement. Frameless tempered glass door, two-fan air circulation, LED backlit whiteboard, power outlet, and occupancy sensor — standard. Fully customizable in hundreds of color and material combinations.

Loop Cube

The four-person meeting pod built for collaboration. Same premium construction as the Solo — frameless glass door, active ventilation, integrated power — scaled for small team meetings.

Loop Access

Barrier-free accessibility design without compromise. Full acoustic performance with ADA-compliant layout, designed to accommodate all users with dignity and ease.

Bow Lounge Chair

A refined balance of form and comfort. Wood base with cloth, vinyl, or felt seating options, designed to complement the Loop pod aesthetic throughout the office.

Hardisty Collection

Refined, modular, made to fit. Love seats and seating with walnut base and customizable upholstery for executive lounges and collaborative zones.

Glenora Planter

Adds greenery and flexibility to any interior. Designed to integrate biophilic elements naturally alongside Loop pods for a cohesive, calming environment.
